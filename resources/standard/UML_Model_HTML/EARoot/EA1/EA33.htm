To avoid large coordinates, an offset of the whole dataset may be applied using the #lt;offset#gt; element. It enables inertial relocation and re-orientation of datasets. The dataset is first translated by @x, @y, and @z. Afterwards, it is rotated by @hdg around the new origin. Rotation around the z-axis should be avoided.In OpenDRIVE, the offset of a database is represented by the #lt;offset#gt; element within the #lt;header#gt; element.<br/>
