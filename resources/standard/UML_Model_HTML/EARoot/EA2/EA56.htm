Appears In: Junction
If an incoming road is linked to an outgoing road with multiple connection roads to represent several possible lane connections, then one of these connections may be prioritized. Assigning a priority is only required if the application is unable to derive priorities from signals before or inside a junction or from the lanes leading to a junction. At least one attribute must be given.<br/><br/>
